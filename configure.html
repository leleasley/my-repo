How it works

What LeLibrary does

Reads your TorBox downloads, groups episodes into series, fetches TMDB metadata, and serves it all as a Stremio/Nuvio addon.

  • Movies, Series, and Anime — three separate catalogs
  • Auto-detected anime via TMDB (Japanese + Animation)
  • Rich stream info: quality, codec, HDR, audio, size
  • Direct playback from TorBox CDN
