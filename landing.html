TMDB Metadata

Smart Caching

Redis persistent cache plus in-memory NodeCache. Background refresh every 30 minutes with hash-based invalidation when your downloads change.

Dual Source

Works with TorBox torrents & usenet, plus optional Real-Debrid. Merge both catalogs or keep them completely separate.

01

Grab your API keys

Get your TorBox API key from your account settings. Grab a free TMDB key too. Real-Debrid is optional.

02

Configure the addon

Paste your keys into the config page. Pick your language, sort order, and whether to include Real-Debrid.

03

Install & watch

Hit install for Stremio Desktop, Stremio Web, or Nuvio. Your library appears instantly — organized and enriched.
